Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Student

 Do’s

* Think before posting– Ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
* Protect your privacy– Use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.
* Verify information– Check facts before sharing news or posts.
* Maintain professionalism– Remember that future employers may view your profile.
* Engage positively– Share useful content and communicate respectfully.
* Limit screen time– Balance online and offline activities.

Don’ts

* Don’t share personal details– Avoid posting phone numbers, address, or sensitive data.
* Don’t post inappropriate content– Offensive or harmful posts can affect your reputation.
* Don’t spread misinformation– Avoid forwarding unverified news or rumors.
* Don’t cyberbully – Never engage in harassment or negative comments.
* Don’t accept unknown requests – Be cautious with strangers online.
* Don’t overshare – Keep a healthy boundary between personal and public life.
